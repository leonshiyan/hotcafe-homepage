ABOUT OUR STORE

HOT CAFÉ LTD, ESTABLISHED ON JAN. 2013.

A PLACE TO ENJOY A CUP OF AUTHENTIC CHINESE TEA, NOT AN AWFUL TEA BAG.
ORIGINALLY, OUR STORE ESTABLISHED IN 1937 IN "JING DE ZHEN", XIANGXI PROVINCE, CHINA, WHICH IS NOT ONLY WELL KNOWN OF THE CAPITAL OF PORCELAIN OR CHINA OVER THE WORLD, BUT ALSO CHINESE TEA. THE BLACK TEA PRODUCED IN THAT AREA IS KNOWN AS "QI HONG". IN 1917，"QI HONG" BLACK TEA WAS AWARDED A GOLD MEDAL IN PANAMA INTERNATIONAL FAIR.
THE FOUNDER OF OUR TEA STORE WAS MR. WANG WHO HAS BEEN IN PARIS FOR 11 YEARS AND WORKED AS A COFFEE STORE WAITER. AFTER COMING BACK TO HIS HOMETOWN, MR WANG ESTABLISHED THE VERY FIRST STORE "红茶馆". IN HIS TEA HOUSE, MR WANG CREATED A NEW BLENDED BLACK TEA WHICH TEASTE WAS VERY SIMILAR AS COFFEE. SOON AFTER, LOCAL PEOPLE BEGIN TO CALL IT CHINESE COFFEE TEA, NOT BLACK TEA.
"红茶馆" WAS VERY WELCOME BY LOCAL PEOPLE UNTIL 1949 WHEN WANG'S FAMILY WAS EXILED OVERSEA.
95 YEARS LATER, WANG'S OFFSPRING BRINGS THIS LEGEND CHINESE TEA TO VANCOUVER TO CONTINUE THE STORY WHICH STARTS WITH THE SAME NAME OF "HOT", MEANS: HOUSE OF TEA.
MEANWHILE, SOME INNOVATED CHINESE FOODS ARE INTRODUCED TO THIS NEW STORE TO SATISFY OUR MODERN CUSTOMERS.
WITH SO MANY SIMPLE, AFFORDABLE, INTERESTING AND DELICIOUS FOODS, YOU WILL NEVER FEEL DIFFICULT TO HAVE A WONDERFUL BRUNCH OR LUNCH OR NIGHT MEAL, OR SIMPLY JUST HAVE A CUP OF VARIOUS HEALTHY DRINKS IN OUR STORE.
ENJOY.

关于我们的店
红茶馆公司创建于2013年1月。可我们的原始店，追溯至1917年，由王氏家族先人，在江西景德镇创建的"红茶馆"。
闻名于世的瓷都江西景德镇，不仅出好瓷，还出好茶。这里出产的红茶与安徽祁门地区出产的红茶，并称祁红茶。祁红茶在1917年巴拿马世界博览会上荣获金奖，是世界最好的三大红茶之一。
原始店的创始人王先生年轻的时候曾在法国勤工俭学11年，期间在咖啡馆做工，并非常喜爱法国咖啡。1917年，王先生返回中国后，在他的家乡创建了一间红茶店，兼营咖啡，取名"红茶馆"。他在红茶的制作流程中，借鉴了咖啡制作的心得，独创出浓郁咖啡香型的红茶饮料，后人誉之"中华咖啡"或"中华咖啡红茶"。
1949年后，王氏家族流散到海外，中华咖啡红茶在景德镇当地失传，直至后人来到温哥华，创建了新的"红茶馆"。今天的红茶馆，崇尚现代简餐理念，将博大精深的中华茶文化与文明干净快捷的西餐文化融会贯通，为客人提供丰富多彩和价廉物美的现代中华美食，款款精良，应有尽有。
